Welcome to the oauth-sample-v2. This app will show a basic demo of the 'Sign in with Twitter' experience using OAuth 2.0

To get started, we'll need you to authorize oauth-sample-v2 to access your Twitter account. Learn more information about OAuth 2.0

Here's our plan:

  1. We will redirect you to Twitter.com for you to authorize our app.
  2. While at Twitter.com, you will authorize oauth-sample-v2 and you'll then be redirected back to us.
  3. When authorized succesfully, we'll make an authenticated call to the Twitter API to get some information about the user who authorized the app, to show we are able to retrieve this data on your behalf.
